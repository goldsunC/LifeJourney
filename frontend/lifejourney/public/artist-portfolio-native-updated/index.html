About the Artist

Creating art that challenges
the way we see the world

Elena Mori is an award-winning contemporary artist whose work explores the intersection of reality and imagination. With a background in both traditional painting and digital media, she creates multi-layered pieces that invite viewers to question their perceptions.

Her work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe, Asia, and North America, including solo shows at the MoMA PS1, Tate Modern, and Tokyo Contemporary Art Museum. Critics have praised her ability to blend classical techniques with cutting-edge technology.
